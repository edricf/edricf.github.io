Credit Card Fraud Detector

In this project, we are given 28 features which are components outputted from PCA, Time, Amount and wether the transaction is Fraud or Not. Our approach in this project is first scaling our variables to ensure they give the same impact to classifying Fraud and then visualizing our data via PCA/Factor Analysis to see if their are any anomalies and see the underlying trend of our data.

We tested out 3 supervised models particularly Logistic Regression, Support Vector Classifier and 3-layer Deep Neural Network and also 1 Unsupervised Learning model, Gaussian Mixture model.
