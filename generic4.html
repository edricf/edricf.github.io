Fake News Detector App

I first preprocessed the data by Tokenizing the title and text of the news article. I then fitted a LSTM model and compared to a baseline Logistic Regression Model. Saving the models, I then created an app by integrating it with Flask. The accuracy of both models are both good with LSTM having a 99% testing accuracy and Logistic Regression having a 98% testing accuracy
